Cue Magazine

Cue magazine is a prototype film magazine. It would be released monthly and would include: an in depth analysis of an upcoming film, a movie poster/synopsis spread of newly released films, a title sequence breakdown, & articles involving the movie industry. In this particular issue, I included: Prometheus, The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo, & an article from Roger Ebert. Typeset in Bembo Std & Akzidenz-Grotesk BQ. Perfect Bound. Printed on Mohawk glossy paper.

Curator

Fall 2011

In order to develop Curator I had to first confront the task which involved designing a service that organized and maintained the art collection at the Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum in Boston, MA.

I came up with the idea of a to-do list after attending the museum and noticing that it's layout, although beautiful isn't exactly intuitive. Curator would act as an organized guide for the museum visitor, making sure they can view, save, queue, annotate & locate they're desired art pieces from any museum.
